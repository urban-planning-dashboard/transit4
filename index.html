Transit4

Allston development — transit forecast

Four-step travel-demand forecasts for Boston (2019 → 2040), comparing the regional projection against an Allston intensification scenario (Harvard ERC + Beacon Park Yard, served by West Station). Anchored to US Census (LODES jobs/workers + decennial households, CTPS zones) and calibrated to NHTS / ACS / NTD.
